2. Phone Accelerometer for Mouse Control

Overview: This project uses a phone accelerometer to track and mimic mouse movements, enabling users to control their computer with mouse gestures. While the technology is still in development, it explores new possibilities in human-computer interaction and accessibility.

Key Features:
  • Convenient Control: Use subtle mouse movements to navigate and interact with your computer.
  • Ongoing Development: Continuously improving accuracy and responsiveness to ensure optimal user experience.
Technology Used: Accelerometer Sensors, Machine Learning, Kotlin, Python, Data Gathering

3. AI-Generated Music for Enhanced Audiobooks

Overview: This project enhances the audiobook experience by pairing each chapter, verse, or even entire books with AI-generated background music tailored to the emotional tone of the text. Currently applied to the Bible, this project aims to improve accessibility for people across different languages and cultures. The music adapts to the emotional highs and lows of the content, offering a more immersive and engaging listening experience.

Key Features:
  • Emotion-Driven Music Composition: AI generates music that aligns with the emotional undertones of the content.
  • Immersive Audiobook Experience: Offers a dynamic listening environment for better engagement and comprehension.
  • Multilingual Accessibility: Focuses on providing enriched Bible experiences in multiple languages.
Technology Used: Natural Language Processing (NLP), AI Music Generation, Emotion Detection, automated uploading
